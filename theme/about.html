Our Story

SBA Consulting CPA (formerly Acosta Tax & Advisory, PA) is a distinguished tax and accounting firm specializing in comprehensive back-office solutions designed specifically for small to medium-sized businesses. Established in 2018 as a boutique firm in Hollywood, Florida, with a primary focus on Tax Preparation and Planning, we quickly recognized a common challenge faced by business owners. Managing accounting, tax compliance, and financial strategies while simultaneously handling day-to-day operations proved to be a daunting task. Many businesses deferred crucial accounting activities, only completing them to meet tax filing obligations, which posed significant risks and hindered their growth potential.

Late bill payments often resulted in frequent assessments of substantial penalties, squandering funds that could have been better invested in operational endeavors. Our firm witnessed these financial hardships faced by businesses, particularly concerning filing returns, overdue tax payments, or audits related to income, payroll, or sales taxes. Few businesses proactively implemented tax and financial plans to minimize liabilities and enhance their bottom line, missing out on strategies such as contributing to tax-deferred retirement plans or optimizing their business structure.

In response, our firm strategically evolved to become an indispensable partner, offering a comprehensive and flexible service package that proactively addresses these challenges. Through a fixed monthly fee, we deliver an integrated approach encompassing bookkeeping, accounts payable, payroll, tax return preparation and filing, business financial reporting, and tax and financial planning. Leveraging cutting-edge software applications and an online portal, we embrace technology to grant business owners immediate access to their tax and financial information. Our proactive approach entails regular client check-ins to understand their evolving needs. Today, our firm provides business owners with peace of mind, freeing up their time and resources from non-revenue generating activities, enabling them to focus on executing strategies that enhance operations, foster business growth, and maximize their profitability.
