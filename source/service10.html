Incorporating as an S Corp

Discover the power of forming a C Corporation! With this structure, your business can accommodate unlimited investors and diverse stock types. Enjoy the benefits of a separate tax-paying entity, while we guide you through the process. Embrace boundless possibilities and unlock your business's potential with our C Corporation formation services.

To qualify for S corporation status, your business must meet certain standards and qualifications. The CPAs at My CPA, PA, are happy to help you decide if this incorporation option is right for you.

If you choose to be a C Corporation, you may need assistance filing the following forms:

  • Income Tax Forms (1120)
  • Estimated Tax Forms (1120W)
  • Employment Tax Forms (941, 943, W2, W3, 1099/1096s, UCT6)

By establishing a C Corporation you will be able to protect your personal assets from your business, and while there are many online companies that allow for you to incorporate by yourself we always advise to seek out professional help.
