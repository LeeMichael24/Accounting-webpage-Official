Incorporating as an S Corp

Every entity, whether domestic or foreign, that conducts business in the United States is required to have a registered agent (RA) and a registered office. The registered office must have a physical street address within the United States and cannot be a P.O. box. This applies to corporations, limited liability companies (LLCs), and limited partnerships, including those owning real estate or holding mortgages on U.S. properties.
